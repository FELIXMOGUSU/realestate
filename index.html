How the platform works

The experience is customized for stays, rentals, and property purchase while protecting privacy where needed.

1

Clients browse homes beautifully

Search by type, city, price, and bedrooms. Enjoy a premium landing experience designed to convert interest into action.

2

Privacy rules match listing type

Airbnb and sale listings can show full information. Rental listings hide the exact address until agent-assisted viewing is requested.

3

Owners grow with simple pricing

Owners list 5 houses free. Once they exceed that limit, they move to the annual KSh 5,000 listing plan.
